Let's be honest about 3 weeks.

Safe fat loss is 0.5–1 kg per week, so realistic fat loss is about 2–2.5 kg. The scale will likely move 3–4 kg because you also drop water and glycogen early. Chasing much more than that is the aggressive edge — possible on the scale, but pushing harder risks losing muscle and bouncing back. Aim for steady ~0.5–0.75 kg/week and treat anything beyond as a bonus. Health first — always.

How this actually works

Three levers, in order of impact. Master the first two; the third accelerates them.

Lever 1 · biggest

Eat at a small deficit

Fat only leaves when you eat a bit less energy than you burn. ~1,650 kcal/day does that without starving. See the food plan →

Lever 2 · protective

Keep protein high

~170 g/day keeps you full and signals your body to burn fat, not muscle. Every meal is built around it. Why →

Lever 3 · accelerator

Train + move daily

Dumbbell strength + core + steps widen the deficit and tighten your midsection. See the program →

Start today — 5 minutes

  • Weigh yourself in the morning and log it in the Tracker. This is your day-0 number.
  • Take one photo in the mirror. The scale lies on bad days; photos don't.
  • Skim the Theory page once so the food plan makes sense.
  • Print this week's Shopping List and stock the kitchen.

  • Fill a 1.5 L bottle and aim to drink two of them today.
